Our Story

Founded in the heart of Huntingdonshire, Sub, Co. represents a new chapter in British tea culture. We believe that exceptional tea should be accessible to those who appreciate the finer things in life.

Our master blenders work tirelessly to source the finest leaves from across the globe, creating blends that honor tradition while embracing innovation. Every cup tells a story of craftsmanship, quality, and the timeless ritual of tea.

We are committed to sustainability, working directly with tea gardens to ensure fair trade practices and environmental responsibility. Because great tea should benefit everyone involved in its journey.
